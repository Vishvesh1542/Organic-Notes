## Reduction of Alkyne to Cis Alkene

- We have 3 reagents for this

$\ce{R-C#C-R' ->[H_{2}-Pd, CaCO_{3}][in Xyelen, S or Quinol]}$ %%qf%% $\ce{R-CH=CH-R' ( cis alkene)}$^lindlarreductionofalkyne

- This is called **Lindlar Catalyst**

$\ce{R-C#C-R' ->[H_{2}-Pd, BaSO_{4}][in Xyelen, S or Quinol]}$ %%qf%% $\ce{R-CH=CH-R' ( cis alkene)}$^rosenmundreductionofalkyne

- This is called **Rosenmund Catalyst**

$\ce{R-C#C-R' ->[Ni_{2}B]}$ %%qf%% $\ce{R-CH=CH-R' ( cis alkene)}$^P2reductionofalkyne

- This is called **P-2 Catalyst**

- Keep in mind This is ONLY for **Non terminal Alkynes**, terminal alkynes don't do this

## Reduction of Acid Halide to Aldehyde

- Rosenmund and Lindlar also work here

$\ce{R-C(=O)-Cl ->[H_{2}-Pd, CaCO_{3}][in Xyelen, S or Quinol]}$ %%qf%% $\ce{R-C(=O)-H}$^lindlarreductionofacidhalide

$\ce{R-C(=O)-Cl ->[H_{2}-Pd, BaSO_{4}][in Xyelen, S or Quinol]}$ %%qf%% $\ce{R-C(=O)-H}$^Rosenmundreductionofacidhalide
